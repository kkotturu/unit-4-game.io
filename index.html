Crystal Collector Game

Instructions
  1. At the start of each round, you will be given a random number. That number is your goal.
  2. There are four crystals below. Each crystal is worth a specific amount of points. By clicking on a crystal, you will add its value to your total score.
  3. To win the game, match your total score to the goal. If your total score goes above the goal, you lose.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it, and the values will be changed with each new round.
    5. Good luck!
